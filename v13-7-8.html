About Our Company

CT Plastic Recycling Industry Pvt. Ltd. is an innovative startup in Bardiya, Nepal, pioneering sustainable plastic recycling solutions. As a new entrant in the industry, we're establishing our operations with a pilot-scale processing capacity of 50 kg per hour, focusing on quality and environmental impact.

We specialize in transforming post-consumer PET plastic waste into valuable raw materials including plastic flakes and pellets. Our compact facility utilizes modern recycling technology to ensure consistent quality while maintaining flexibility to serve local market needs.

With a commitment to environmental stewardship, we're building the foundation for scalable plastic recycling solutions in Nepal, starting with our pilot operation that serves as a model for future expansion.
